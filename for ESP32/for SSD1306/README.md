## List of Pins

|SSD1306 Display	|Type of Pin on ESP32 |

|VCC			|3v3	 	           |
|D0			|32	     		    |
|D1			|33			    |
|RESET			|25		     	    |
|DC			|26			    |
|CS			|27			    |
